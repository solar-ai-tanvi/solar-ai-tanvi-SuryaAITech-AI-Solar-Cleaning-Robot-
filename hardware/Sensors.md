# 📡 Sensors Documentation

## Camera Module

Purpose:
Capture solar panel images for AI vision processing.

---

## Ultrasonic Sensor

Purpose:
Detect obstacles in front of the robot.

---

## IR Sensors

Purpose:
Detect panel edges and prevent the robot from falling.

---

## Battery Voltage Sensor

Purpose:
Continuously monitor battery voltage.

---

## Water Level Sensor

Purpose:
Monitor available water level inside the tank.

---

All sensors are connected to ESP32 except the Camera Module, which is connected to Raspberry Pi.
