# ⚙️ Motors Documentation

## Drive Motors

Quantity: 2

Purpose:
Move the robot forward and backward.

---

## Roller Brush Motor

Quantity: 1

Purpose:
Rotate the cleaning brush.

---

## Water Pump

Quantity: 1

Purpose:
Spray water before cleaning when required.

---

All motors are controlled through the BTS7960 Motor Driver by ESP32.
